How to Download YouTube Videos for Free (Phone & PC)

Saving a video for offline viewing is handy when you travel, have unstable internet, or want to keep a copy of your own published videos. Here's how to do it for free, with nothing to install.

⚠️ Use this guide only with your own videos, public domain content, or material you have permission to download. Respecting copyright is your responsibility.

The easy method: an online downloader

  1. Copy the video address from the browser bar or the Share button.
  2. Open TubeFlow and paste the link into the tool.
  3. Choose MP4 and your preferred quality (720p is the best balance of quality and file size).
  4. Hit download. Done.

Which quality should you pick?

  • 360p: small files, great for phones with limited storage.
  • 720p (HD): recommended for most cases.
  • 1080p (Full HD): best quality, larger files.

Tips

  • For long videos, use Wi-Fi to save mobile data.
  • On a computer, files go to your browser's Downloads folder.
  • Only need the audio? See our YouTube to MP3 guide.
